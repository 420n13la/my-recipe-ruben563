My breakfest recipe!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Bagel
  • Oil
  • Butter
    •

Steps

  • Pour some oil on your pan
  • Crack your eggs and put them on your pan
  • Put your stove on medium
  • Put your bagel in the toaster and toast them
  • Bake your bacon on another pan
  • wait fifteen minutes
  • pour your eggs and bacon on your bagel

    • Eat well!! :)
